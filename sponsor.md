## What Is Echoes of Order?

Echoes of Order is a **systems-driven, persistent online world** (MMO-style) built with **authority and simulation** at the centre: one source of truth per realm, replaceable simulations that propose outcomes but never own state. The goal is a coherent, long-lived world where infrastructure and correctness come first — not a theme-park MMO or a quick prototype.

## Why This Is Unique

- **Authority-first:** The realm is the single source of truth. Simulations propose; the authority validates. Few public projects document this split at this depth.
- **Transparent:** Decisions and explorations are written down and published. Reasoning is traceable.
- **Independent:** No investors, no feature pressure. Correctness and structure over speed.
- **Long-term:** Code and documentation are maintained so the work stays useful even as the game evolves slowly.
