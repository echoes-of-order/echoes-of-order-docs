# Custom Spell Creation (Morrowind-Inspired)

**Status:** Active

## Problem / Idea

I explored an optional system that lets players create custom abilities (e.g. combining effects, tuning cost and power), inspired by Morrowind’s spell creation. The goal was depth for enthusiasts without forcing complexity on everyone: standard abilities remain sufficient for the full game; custom creation is optional and carries cost and risk.

## Points Considered

- Optionality: default abilities are enough to complete the game; custom creation is a prestige path.
- Costs: creation cost (e.g. gold or equivalent) vs. usage cost (e.g. mana); no double-punishment.
- Risk and stability: stronger custom abilities could have lower success chance or higher failure cost to avoid trivial “god” builds.
- Learning curve: mechanical depth and transparency so that mastery is possible without being mandatory.
- Balance: impact on PvE and PvP; tuning levers (success rate, cost, cooldowns).

## Outcome

Under evaluation. Custom ability creation is a candidate feature. No final design or implementation timeline yet.
