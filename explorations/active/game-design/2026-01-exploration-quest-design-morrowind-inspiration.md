# Quest Design (Morrowind-Inspired) — Active Exploration

**Status:** Active (direction accepted; see Accepted for outcome)

## Problem / Idea

I explored how to design quests for a persistent, consequential world: story-driven content, long-form text where it adds value, multiple paths and consequences, and support for both narrative-focused and action-focused players (e.g. via short summaries alongside full text).

## Points Considered

- Inspiration from Morrowind: long dialogues, faction and political stakes, lasting consequences.
- Quest types: main story, faction/guild, side quests; how they affect the world and the player.
- Accessibility: short summaries for players who prefer action; optional full text and future features like text-to-speech.
- Balance: depth and immersion vs. clarity and pacing; integration with the rest of the world systems.

## Outcome

This direction has been **accepted** as the target for quest design. The full decision and implications are documented under [Accepted: Quest Design (Morrowind-Inspired)](../../accepted/gameplay/2026-01-exploration-quest-design-morrowind-inspiration.md). Implementation will follow.
